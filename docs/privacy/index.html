Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Certainty Home Consulting ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or submitting your information to us, you agree to the practices described below.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us. This typically includes:

  • Your name and email address when you request our free guide or contact us.
  • Your phone number, only if you choose to provide it.
  • Details you share about your renovation project when you book or inquire about a consultation.
  • Basic, anonymous usage data (such as pages visited) through standard website analytics.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Send you the free guide and any materials you requested.
  • Respond to your questions and follow up about a consultation.
  • Provide our advisory services and communicate with you about them.
  • Improve our website and the help we offer homeowners.

We do not sell your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. We do not share it with contractors or third parties for their own marketing.

Third-party services

We use trusted services to run our business, and your information may pass through them only for that purpose:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram): If you submit a form through one of our ads, Meta collects your details and shares them with us. Meta's use of your data is governed by its own Privacy Policy.
  • Email and scheduling tools we use to deliver the guide and arrange consultations.
  • Website analytics to understand general site traffic.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop contacting you. Just email us and we'll take care of it promptly.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Email todd@certaintyhomeconsulting.com and we'll respond.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
